# 🔮 Secret Oracle

The Oracle holds a secret image. You cannot see it. You guess what it is.

---

## What is the Oracle?

The Secret Oracle is an AI-powered guessing game. At the start of each round the Oracle picks a hidden image, analyzes it, and writes a cryptic hint describing what it sees. Your job is to read that hint and figure out what the image is before you run out of ideas.

You never see the image until the game ends — either because you guessed correctly, or because you gave up.

---

## How to play

1. **Start a game** — press *Enter the Oracle*. The Oracle conjures a hidden image and gives you the first hint.
2. **Type a guess** — describe what you think the image shows and press *Send* or hit `Enter`.
3. **Read the response** — the Oracle tells you whether your guess is correct or not, and may offer a clue about how close you are.
4. **Keep guessing** — you can make as many guesses as you need.
5. **Ask for more hints** — if you're stuck, press *Ask for Hint*. The Oracle will reveal an additional clue.
6. **Give up** — press *Surrender* at any time. The Oracle reveals the image and the answer immediately.
7. **Play again** — press *New Game* to start a fresh round with a brand-new hidden image.

---

## Rules

- The Oracle picks the image. You have no influence over what it chooses.
- There is no guess limit. You can try as many times as you want.
- Hints are limited. Once the Oracle has no hints left it will tell you, and no more will be given.
- A guess is considered correct when the Oracle's AI judges that your answer matches the content of the image. You do not need an exact word — a clear, accurate description is enough.
- Surrendering ends the round immediately. The image and the correct answer are revealed.
- Each new game is independent. Stats (guesses, hints, games played) are tracked per session.
